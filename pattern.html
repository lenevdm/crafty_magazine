DIY face mask

A tutorial on how to make your own fabric face mask.

Tools and materials

  • Needle and thread or sewing machine
  • Scissors
  • Pins or fabric clips
  • At least 30 by 30 cm of cotton fabric
  • Two pieces of 7mm sewing elastic, 20cm long.
  • Printed pattern
